Cable Solutions: Leading the Nordic Market in Recycling and Low-Carbon Innovation

Zirq Solutions excels in cable recycling, utilizing cutting-edge technology to efficiently process complex cables and transformers while minimizing waste. Through our commitment to the circular economy, we transform mixed plastic waste into valuable recycled materials, leading the way in sustainable cable solutions.

High-quality Cable Recycling

Our state-of-the-art facilities and advanced technology enable us to efficiently recycle complex cables and transformers, setting industry standards for sustainability.

Clean Power Generation

We actively promote the use of renewable energy sources in our cable recycling processes, reducing our carbon footprint and contributing to a greener future.

Circular Solutions

Our innovative approach allows us to transform mixed plastic waste generated from cable recycling into recycled raw materials and commercial products, diverting plastics from landfills and incineration.

Efficient Cable Offloading

With specialized equipment and expertise, we handle the offloading of cables from ships, providing reliable and seamless logistics solutions.

Extensive Coverage

Serving Scandinavia and expanding into Northern Europe, we are well-positioned to meet the recycling and material procurement needs of diverse projects.
