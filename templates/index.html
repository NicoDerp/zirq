Innovating Recycling for a Sustainable Future

Join us in transforming the world's waste problem. Zirq Solutions is at the forefront of revolutionizing recycling, focusing on cable, medical waste, and tackling the global plastic crisis head-on. Our cutting-edge solutions, backed by Norsk Gjenvinning Group, Norway's leading recycling company, make us a trusted supplier of recycled materials to the European industry. Together, let's accelerate the transition to a circular economy for a greener, more sustainable tomorrow.

Zirq Solutions: Driving Circular Waste Management

Zirq Solutions is at the forefront of innovative and industrial recycling solutions, focusing on cable and medical waste. With expertise, advanced technology, and collaboration with NG Metal, we are a trusted supplier of high-quality recycled materials to European industries.

Efficient Recycling

Streamlined Recycling Process: Our advanced facilities and technology enable efficient recycling of complex waste streams, minimizing waste and maximizing resource recovery.

Circular Solutions

Closing the Loop: We develop innovative circular solutions for low-quality mixed plastics and medical waste, transforming them into valuable recycled materials and contributing to a sustainable future.

Collaborative Partnerships

Strategic Collaboration: We work closely with industry-leading partners, including Norsk Gjenvinning Group, to drive impactful change and foster a collaborative approach towards a circular economy.
