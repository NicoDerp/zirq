Our Story

Zirq Solutions, headquartered in Norway with 65 employees in Norway and Denmark, is a leading provider of sustainable recycling solutions. With main production sites in Linnestad, Norway and Stenlille, Denmark, as well as two smaller sites in Præstø and Ølgod, we are strategically positioned to deliver efficient and effective recycling processes.

  • Industry-leading knowledge and expertise drive our innovative recycling solutions.
  • Strong partnerships amplify our efforts for positive environmental change.
  • Transforming waste into valuable resources for a greener future.
  • Efficient operations at scale ensure effective recycling and resource conservation.

At Zirq Solutions, we are dedicated to making a significant impact on the circular economy through sustainable recycling practices. With our experienced team and state-of-the-art facilities, we strive to provide scalable solutions that address the pressing challenges of waste management and contribute to a more sustainable future for generations to come.

Sustainable Footprint

Our commitment to sustainability is reflected in our operations' ecological footprint, including resource consumption, greenhouse gas emissions, and environmental impacts. Through our recycling efforts, we save thousands of tonnes of CO2 annually. As we continuously strive to reduce our own emissions, we aim to replace virgin raw materials with recycled alternatives for a greener future.

Resource Conservation

Reduced Resource Consumption: By utilizing our recycled aluminium, copper, and plastic materials, you contribute to resource conservation, minimizing the need for extracting virgin raw materials and preserving natural resources.

Emissions Reduction

Lowered Greenhouse Gas Emissions: The use of our recycled materials significantly reduces CO2 equivalents, helping to mitigate climate change and lower overall carbon emissions.
